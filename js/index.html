Stay Safe, Stay at Home

These steps can keep yourself safe from deadly corona virus.

Always Carry Sanitizer

Always carry sanitizer in your pocket while you're going out for your extreme needs

Wash Hands Properly

Wash your hands properly after every 2 hours and also when you enter your home from outside

Always Wear Mask

Never ever go outside without wearing a mask. The virus is spreading via nose & mouth. So always cover it with a mask

No Social Gathering

Except emergency, do not go outside. If you go outside do not make yourself present in any kind of social gathering

Maintain Social Distance

Whenever you're present in any public place, always maintain social distancing of about 1 meters. You'll be safe from contamination

Cough in Elbow

If you're not carring any handkerchief, then while you cough, always cough in your elbow so that there's no fear of spreading the virus
